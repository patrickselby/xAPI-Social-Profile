ADL xAPI Social Collaboration Hang out - Link

This community is designed to explore uses of the XAPI specification to track learner's social activities with a goal of understanding the needs of managers or instructors trying to track their users interests, informal learning experience for transcript completeness, and social awareness. Since the core of this is "Social"; we'll use the "social medium" to collaborate. The Social CoP is a community activity open to anyone with interest in the development and sharing knowledge and experimenting with the creation and use of Social experiences.
